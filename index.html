About me

Short presentation

My name is Olle Holmgren and i am studying interactionsdesign at Linnaeus University in Växjö. I like to combine design and technology to create solutions that feel both stylish and user friendly. I am curious, calm and practially minded person who loves to learn new things and take on challenges. I like to see things shape, whether it is about code, layout or a physical project.

Objectives

My goal is to keep developing my skills within webdevelopment and interaction design, and to create digital experinces that feel meaningful, intuitive and well-crafted. I want to work in the intersection between design and technology, where i can combine creativity with problem solving. In the long run, I aim to work on projects that have a clear user focus and contribute to something positive. I'm motivated by seeing ideas grow into something real and by constatly learning new tools, methods and perspectives.
